Website Evaluations

Raycon Global

Raycon global is an earbud and headphones producer who do most of their business
online. They include plenty of links to navigate their site which is easy to inderstand
at a glance and pleasant to look at.

  • Contrast: A bright background and dark text make it easy to read and orange hovered links make them stand out well.
  • Repetition: The site is in split into sections which repeat header and body font in a way which makes sense.
  • Alignment: The website is aligned in a way which makes sense such as lists and links in the header being aligned left and body content being aligned center.
  • Proximity: Everything is in a place which makes sense and nothing feels out of place or disjointed.

Bonzi Buddy

Bonzi Buddy is a download site for an internet helper (virus) which is supposed to make
browsing the web easier. The website is covered in animated gifs of the purple monkey
Bonzi and the bullet points are even bananas. How nice.

  • Contrast: The contrast on the page is mostly fine, everything is readable though pruple on green is a bit grating.
  • Repetition: All of the basic content text is fairly repetitious with the same font though almost every logo and image is different leading to a very unhinged page.
  • Alignment: Text on the page is aligned center except the odd paragraph or two making them feel out of place. Images everywhere, in the center, on the sides, in the corners.
  • Proximity: Download links are located at the top and bottom of the page leading to confusion while information about the program itself is scattered through the page.
